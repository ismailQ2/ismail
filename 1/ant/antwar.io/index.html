    About


    AntWar.io is a fun multiplayer IO game where you play as an ant. Defend your colony and defeat your enemies. Build your ant empire and work with other players to win.


    Forage for food, hunt for bugs, defend your queen, and grow your colony. AntWar.io is inspired a bit by other ant games, but a lot of the inspiration comes from ants in real life. Ant wars, aphid farming, etc. are all something ants do naturally!
    If you are interested in learning more about ants or ant keeping check out r/antkeeping on Reddit.


    AntWar.io is owned and developed by Bombsight Games. We can be contacted there or by emailing contact@bombsightgames.com.

    How to Play


    Controls
    1. Tap/click or use WASD to move, right click(or double tap) to perform an action. (Like picking up food, attacking, found colony, etc.)
    2. Use left click, ctrl, or press down on your ant to open the command menu.
    3. Press enter to chat.
    Starting New Colony
    1. When you start a new colony, you will be the queen.
    2. Find a good spot for your nest that is near food, humid(dark) dirt, and dry(light) dirt.
    3. You can see small hints on the surface for humid and dry dirt.
    4. Right click(or double tap) somewhere on the ground to dig and start your colony.
    Growing your Colony
    1. Teamwork is key, communicate with your fellow players!
    2. Ensure your queen and larvae are fed. Pick up food (green balls) and place it on your queen/larvae when they are hungry(white icon over her head). AI will do this too.
    3. Place eggs/larvae/pupae on dark wet dirt and food on light dry dirt underground.
    4. Dig for more space by double tapping on dirt tiles, you must place dirt outside.
    5. Collect food from the surface to feed your colony.
    6. Spiders drop blue food when killed, feed this to larvae to create soldier ants!
    How to Win
    1. Build your colony up to defeat all other queens.
    2. Once all enemy queens are killed, your team will win!
